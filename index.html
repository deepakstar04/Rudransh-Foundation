    About Us

    You can help the poor

    Rudransh Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping those in need. Our mission is to provide food, shelter, and medical support to families and individuals who are struggling. We believe that everyone deserves a chance to live a better life, and we are committed to making a difference in the world.

    Get Help

    If you or someone you know needs assistance, we are here to help.

    Food Aid

    Food aid is a way to provide food to people in need, usually in developing countries. It can be in the form of cash, food vouchers, or in-kind food.

    Shelter Assistance

    Shelter assistance is a process that provides safe and adequate housing to people in need. It can include emergency shelter, cash assistance, and support for long-term housing.

    Medical Support

    Medical support can refer to financial assistance, external intervention, or a parent's obligation to pay for their child's medical care
